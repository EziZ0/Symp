About The Symposium

Dextro'25 is hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Business Systems at R.M.D Engineering College , providing students with a diverse range of opportunities to test their skills and knowledge in the field of technology.

The Department of CSBS

Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) is an academic program that integrates the field of Computer Science and Business. This curriculum aims to ensure that the students graduating from the program not only know the core topics of Computer Science but also develop an equal appreciation of Humanities, Management Sciences, and Human Values. The students are also exposed to emerging topics such as Analytics, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, the Internet of Things, etc., to make them industry ready at the end of four years of study. The students are also exposed to pursue Higher Studies in Reputed Universities Abroad and India with varied specializations and to become a successful Entrepreneur in the IT domain with Innovative Business Skills acquired through the competent curriculum designed by TCS.

Our College

R.M.D Engineering College, a renowned institution consistently ranked among the top in Tamil Nadu, was established with a vision of delivering exceptional education. With strong industry collaborations, the Institute has made a remarkable global impact.
